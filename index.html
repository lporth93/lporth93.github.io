Biography

The development of this website is still in progress!

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Argelander Institut fuer Astronomie (AIfA) at the University of Bonn where I am working in the field of observational cosmology. I obtained my PhD at the Astronomy Centre of the University of Sussex and my Bachelor’s (2014) and Master’s (2017) degree in physics at the LMU Munich.

You can check out my complete CV here and my publication list here.
